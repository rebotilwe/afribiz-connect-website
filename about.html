Our Story

Urbanisation & Development Through Technology

Afribiz Connect is a distinguished international, multi-award-winning IT agency proudly operating from South Africa. We are a diverse collective of specialized experts in Digital Software Development, AI & Machine Learning, and Information Systems Architecture, structured to deliver cutting-edge technology solutions.

Our power is rooted in the strategic fusion of advanced technological knowledge, including Data Engineering, Cloud Architecture, and Cybersecurity, with deep local expertise. We guarantee that our clients are equipped with all the essential elements necessary for digital dominance.

Afribiz Connect holds deep roots and a proud commitment to supporting SMMEs and local businesses, particularly within South African townships, through technology enablement and digital transformation.
